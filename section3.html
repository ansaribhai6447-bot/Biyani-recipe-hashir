Plain Numbered Steps (Easy to Follow)

1. Marinate the chicken

Clean and pat-dry the chicken pieces. Mix yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, spices, lemon juice, mint, coriander, and fried onions. Marinate for 1–4 hours.

2. Soak the rice

Rinse basmati rice until water runs clear. Soak for 30 minutes.

3. Make birista (fried onions)

Thinly slice onions, fry golden, and set aside.

4. Parboil the rice

Cook rice 70% with spices, drain and keep aside.

5. Cook the chicken

Cook marinated chicken in oil/ghee with spices until nearly done.

6. Prepare saffron milk

Soak saffron strands in warm milk. Add rose/kewra water if desired.

7. Layering for dum

Layer chicken, rice, fried onions, mint, coriander, saffron milk, and ghee.

8. Seal the pot & cook on dum

Seal tightly, cook on low heat for 25–35 minutes.

9. Rest and open

Let rest 10–15 minutes before opening.

10. Serve

Serve hot with raita, salad, and garnish.

Chef’s Tips

  • Use aged basmati rice for best results.
  • Turmeric + milk can replace saffron for color.
  • Heavy pot avoids burning during dum.

Quick Timing Summary

  • Prep: 30–45 minutes
  • Marination: 1–4 hours
  • Cooking: 45–60 minutes
