It's helpful to divide the ingredients by their use in the recipe.

     For Marinating the Chicken:

  • Chicken: 500g, cut into medium-sized pieces (bone-in for more flavor)
  • Yogurt: 1/2 cup, whisked
  • Ginger-Garlic Paste: 1.5 tablespoons
  • Lemon Juice: 2 tablespoons
  • Turmeric Powder: 1/2 teaspoon
  • Biryani Masala Powder: 1.5 tablespoons (recommended brands: Shan or Everest)
  • Salt: 1 teaspoon (or to taste)
  • Fresh Mint Leaves: 1/4 cup, chopped
  • Fresh Cilantro (Coriander) Leaves: 1/4 cup, chopped

     For Cooking the Rice:

  • Basmati Rice: 2 cups (soaked for 30 minutes)
  • Water: 8-10 cups, for boiling
  • Whole Spices:
    1. 1 Bay Leaf
    2. 4-5 Green Cardamom Pods
    3. 1-inch Cinnamon Stick
    4. 4-5 Cloves
    5. 1 Star Anise (optional)
  • Salt: 1.5 tablespoons (for the water)
  • Oil or Ghee: 1 tablespoon

     For Layering and "Dum" (Slow Cooking):

  • Onions: 2 large, thinly sliced (for frying)
  • Oil: 1/2 cup (for frying onions)
  • Saffron Strands: A generous pinch, soaked in 2 tablespoons of warm milk
  • Ghee (Clarified Butter): 2 tablespoons
  • Rose Water or Kewra Water: 1 tablespoon (optional, but highly recommended for aroma)
  • Fresh Mint and Cilantro: A handful for garnish
